### Scan Targets (per vertical)

Each playbook has 4-5 scan targets that guide what the Research Agent looks for:

Salon example:
- "competitor salon pricing {city}"

- "local wedding season dates {city}"
- "Google reviews sentiment salons near {location}"

Restaurant example:
- "restaurant reviews {city} Google rating"
- "popular food trends {city} 2024"
- "local events festivals {city} food"
- "delivery platform commission rates UberEats Glovo"

### Triggers (automation conditions)

Each playbook has 3-4 triggers that can fire campaigns:

Salon triggers:
- Empty slot 48h (appointment_slot_empty_48h)
- Dormant client (client_no_visit_60d)
- Seasonal peak (seasonal_peak_14d_before)

Gym triggers:
- Dropout risk (member_no_checkin_14d)
- Trial expiring (trial_member_7d_before_expiry)
- Dead class slot (class_booking_below_50pct)

### Campaign Templates

Each playbook maps triggers to channels:

Salon:
- Empty slot filler → WhatsApp
- Dormant win-back → WhatsApp
- Wedding season promo → Instagram

Restaurant:
- Midweek special → WhatsApp
- Review response → Internal
- Event promo → Instagram

================================================================================
                        HOW IT WORKS
================================================================================

1. Business selects their vertical type in Settings
2. When user runs a task (e.g., "Find growth opportunities"):
   - SuperAgent classifies the intent
   - Research Agent gets the playbook for that vertical
   - Research prompt includes:
     - Vertical's pain points
     - Priority scan targets
     - Vertical-specific intelligence focus
   - Strategy Agent gets the playbook:
     - Strategy prompt includes vertical's priorities
     - KPIs are included in the context
     - Recommendations are vertical-specific

3. Results are labeled with vertical context:
   - Opportunities include "playbook: 'Salons & Beauty Studios'"
   - Recommendations mention the vertical context

Example:
- Salon owner: "Find growth opportunities" → Research Agent uses salon playbook
- Restaurant owner: "Find growth opportunities" → Research Agent uses restaurant playbook

Same task, different intelligence → different results.
